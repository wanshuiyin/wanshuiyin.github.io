About me

I am a third-year PhD student in computer science in John Hopcroft Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Shuai Li since Sept. 2022. My research interest lies in diffusion models and reinforcement learning. Previously, I focused on the theoretical analysis for diffusion models and the RL method. Currently, I am interested in the combination of diffusion model and reinforcement learning algorithm, such as using the reinforcement learning method to fine-tune the diffusion and use diffusion models in the offline2online RL method
