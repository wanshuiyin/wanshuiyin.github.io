About me

I am a 4-year PhD student in computer science in John Hopcroft Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Shuai Li since Sept. 2022.
My research interest lies in diffusion models and reinforcement learning. Currently, I am focusing on the application and theoretical analysis of diffusion models (including Sampling complexity, Statistical complexity, and Optimization Perspective). I am also interested in the combination of diffusion model and reinforcement learning algorithm, such as using the reinforcement learning method to fine-tune the diffusion and use diffusion models in the offline2online RL method
